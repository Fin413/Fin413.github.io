About me

My name is Finlay Soehn and I'm in my second year of a Software Enginnering degree at the University of Alberta. I have always wanted to work with DiscoverE, having participated in the summer camps as a child.

I have extensive experience with problem-solving and conflict resolution, especially involving younger kids and their parents from my work as a Lifeguard at the Millenium Place Recreational Facility. I would often have to resolve complaints brought up by parents and tactfully ensure the younger children were safe and cautious without hindering their fun. Additionally, because of my lifeguard experience, I have advanced first aid training, including CPR, AED, and O2 Administration with plenty of experience using it.

From my personal experience in these camps as well as from independent experimentation, I am familiar with many of the technologies employed in the camps. I also have experience with the Google Suite of tools and Eclass from previous academic exposure. Additionally, I am fairly familiar with database management as a result of my 6 years of experience in web development and related backend/database technologies.

I work very well in team environments and enjoy collaborative project development. I have a natural curiosity and excel at learning new skills and relevant technologies. I am an excellent problem solver and am self-driven when it comes to accomplishing tasks or finding solutions. It would be a pleasure to learn and share some of my expertise with you. Thank you for your time and consideration.
